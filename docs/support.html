Getting Started

When you first open the app, the onboarding wizard walks you through setting up your company name, adding properties, and connecting Dropbox. You can change all of these later in Settings.

How do I connect Dropbox?

Go to Settings, enter your Dropbox App Key (from dropbox.com/developers), and tap Connect. Set the base path to your Dropbox folder, e.g. "/Real Estate Investing/Taxes/2026".

How do I set up AI assistance?

In Settings under AI Assist, select your provider (Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini) and paste your API key. The app uses your key directly — we never see it.

What if I don't use AI?

The app works great without AI. Apple Vision handles OCR, and the built-in parser extracts vendor, amount, and date from most receipts. AI just helps with hard-to-read ones.

What are the IRS Schedule E categories?

The app uses the 14 standard rental property expense categories from IRS Schedule E: Advertising, Auto/Travel, Cleaning/Maintenance, Commissions, Insurance, Legal/Professional, Management Fees, Mortgage Interest, Other Interest, Repairs, Supplies, Taxes, Utilities, and Depreciation.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free: 2 properties, 10 receipts/month, local storage. Pro: unlimited properties and receipts, Dropbox cloud filing, CSV export, and custom categories.
