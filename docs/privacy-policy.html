Privacy Policy

Receipt Property Pro — Last updated: April 1, 2026

Receipt Property Pro ("the App") is designed to help rental property investors scan, categorize, and file expense receipts. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data Collection

The App processes receipt images and text entirely on your device using Apple Vision framework for OCR. No receipt images or extracted text are sent to our servers. We do not operate any servers that collect, store, or process your data.

Third-Party AI Services (Optional)

If you choose to enable AI-assisted receipt analysis, the App sends receipt images and OCR text directly to the AI provider you select (Anthropic/Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini) using your own API key. These requests go directly from your device to the provider's API. We never see, store, or relay this data. Each provider's own privacy policy governs their handling of your data.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you connect Dropbox, the App uploads receipt PDFs and expense logs directly from your device to your Dropbox account via the Dropbox API. We do not have access to your Dropbox account, tokens, or files. Your OAuth tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device.

On-Device Storage

All app settings, receipt data, expense logs, and scanned images are stored locally on your device. API keys you enter are stored on-device only and are never transmitted to us.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising frameworks, or crash reporting tools. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or behavioral information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any personal data to third parties. The only data that leaves your device is what you explicitly choose to send to your selected AI provider or cloud storage service.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
