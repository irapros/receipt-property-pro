## Description

Receipt Property Pro makes expense tracking effortless for rental property investors and landlords. Scan a receipt with your iPhone camera, and the app extracts the vendor, amount, and date automatically. Assign it to a property, pick a category, and file it — done.

BUILT FOR RENTAL PROPERTY OWNERS
Expenses are organized using IRS Schedule E categories — the same 14 categories on your tax return. Each property gets its own folder structure so everything is ready at tax time.

SMART RECEIPT SCANNING
Apple Vision reads your receipts instantly, right on device. For hard-to-read receipts, connect your own AI provider (Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini) for an extra layer of accuracy. Your API key stays on your device.

AUTO-FILE TO DROPBOX
Connect Dropbox and receipts are saved as PDFs in your existing folder structure. The expense log CSV syncs automatically after every receipt.

DOCUMENT SCANNER
Need to scan something that isn't a receipt? The built-in document scanner saves any multi-page document as a PDF.

FREE TO START
Scan up to 10 receipts per month with 2 properties on the free plan. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited properties, unlimited receipts, cloud filing, and CSV export.

## App Review Notes
This app uses the device camera to scan receipts and documents. OCR is performed on-device using Apple Vision framework. Optional AI features require the user to provide their own third-party API key (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google). Dropbox integration uses OAuth 2.0 PKCE flow. No user data is collected or transmitted to our servers.
